get 10 random jokes:

GET https://backend-test-2021-09.herokuapp.com/api/jokes/

get random jokes of chosen amount (when link opens you can edit amount):

GET https://backend-test-2021-09.herokuapp.com/api/jokes/:amount

get 10 random jokes of chosen category:

GET https://backend-test-2021-09.herokuapp.com/api/jokes/:category

get random jokes of chosen category & amount:

GET https://backend-test-2021-09.herokuapp.com/api/jokes/:category/:amount
